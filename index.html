5 REASONS TO BE THERE

EXTREME WEATHER

NWA is home to 5 types of extreme weather. Local weather experts will teach how to keep your family safe from all of them.

FIRST AID

Unfortunately, kissing it doesn't actually make it better. When it really counts, do you know the first aid needed to make a difference? Come learn the basics of how to handle a medical emergency. These skills can literally be a life-saver!

BASIC VEHICLE MAINTENANCE

When your car won't start, you are officially in an "emergency situation." Don't be sidelined from life because you're not a mechanic. Come learn the basics of keeping your crucial mode of transportation running.

SELF DEFENSE

NWA is relatively safe, but violent crimes or active shooter situations can happen anywhere. Responding properly to these situations can have a critical impact on the outcome, so learn how to protect yourself and your family from someone who might want to do you harm.

GIVE BLOOD (OPENS FRIDAY)

Every 2 seconds, someone in the U.S needs life-saving blood. Donate blood and save a life! Reserve your time-slot in advance to avoid a long wait.
